Cultural Artifact: I will use the TV series Family Guy as my cultural artifact. I will focus on particular episodes within the series that deal directly with the social issue of obesity. 3. The social Issue as mentioned above will be obesity 4. My personal position on the issue is that obesity is a freedom. It is up to the individual to make the healthy choice of not being obese. I believe that through informative action such as education at a young age of the health risks associated with obesity the individual will make an informed decision of not being obese. 5. I believe that the education at a young age will teach kids that being obese is unhealthy and in turn reduce the obesity rate in the long run. As for those already obese and out of school I believe that government paid commercials aimed at informing the masses will make a positive impact to help reduce the obesity rate. Executive Summary The social issue I intend to use is the issue of obesity, I believe that the cultural artifact, Family Guy does a good job in specific episodes exposing the issue. I will analyze how Family Guy’s episodes have an impact on society and how it may alter the perception of its viewers. The purpose of my report will be to show how obesity is unhealthy, that it is a personal choice and a freedom, and how education will help lower the rate of obesity. My main argument will be that through education and informative action we can lower the obesity rate drastically. My position on this issue is that obesity is a personal freedom and though it may be unhealthy and potentially deadly it is the individuals’ freedom to be or not be obese. I do believe that obesity is a health epidemic that is affecting many people. My proposed solution to obesity will be to educate the youth and inform them of the possible health risks through classes in elementary through high schools, we can implement lessons in health classes.
